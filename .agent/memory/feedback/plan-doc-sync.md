Update NEXT.md, TODO.md, PLAN.md after every iteration — not just at the end. Include reflections on what was learned.

**Why:** User expects planning docs to stay in sync as work progresses, not batch-updated at the end. This provides a living record of progress and learnings.

**How to apply:** After completing each task/subtask, immediately update the relevant docs. When reflecting on results (bugs found, design decisions, surprises), add those reflections to PLAN.md or NEXT.md.
